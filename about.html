About Us

The Feed Manthan Story

Welcome to Feed Manthan, India’s premier digital-first Marketing and Election Management agency. While our registered correspondence address is located at R-Block, Patna, Bihar (800001), we operate as a modern, 100% decentralized digital powerhouse. We specialize in high-stakes political consulting, digital infrastructure, and brand scaling, serving clients across the country through a seamless online ecosystem.

The word "Manthan" refers to the deep churning of the ocean to extract the nectar of immortality. Similarly, at Feed Manthan, we believe that success in the digital age requires the deep churning of data, human psychology, and creative innovation. We don't just "post" on social media; we engineer digital movements that shift public perception and drive tangible results.

Our Proven Track Record

We are not newcomers to the field. Feed Manthan has been a silent force behind some of the most intense political battles in the region. Our team has already delivered high-impact results during the previous Bihar Vidhan Sabha Elections. During that period, we managed ground-level digital sentiment, viral content distribution, and real-time candidate branding. Navigating the complex sociopolitical landscape of Bihar has equipped us with the grit, cultural insight, and technical agility to handle any marketing challenge, whether it is a local assembly seat or a national corporate brand launch.
